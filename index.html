My Story

Born with cystic fibrosis but not diagnosed until age 19, I grew up in India, where limited awareness and lack of access to genetic testing meant I lived for years without the full picture of my health — until moving abroad for the first time.

"Travel taught me the art of wonder, and resilience in equal measure."

Through years of navigating hospital stays, procedures, and the uncertainty of living with a chronic rare disease, I’ve learned the power of advocacy and the importance of patient voices in healthcare. I am committed to advancing global access to care, promoting equity in healthcare systems, and highlighting the mental health impact of chronic illness. With my scientific background and lived experience, I bring a unique perspective to patient engagement — working to build stronger connections between patients, clinicians, researchers, and industry to drive meaningful change and inspire hope.
